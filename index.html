Instructions

  • Click on the song you want to hear.
  • While the song is playing, click the "needle" key to pause. This, of course, is like lifting the needle.
  • Click the right arrow key (->) to skip to the next record. Click the left arrow key (<-) to skip to the previous one.
  • If you want to choose a song at random, click the button that will let ol' Jackie decide. He'd be more than happy to do so!
